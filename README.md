The Daily Stop Calulator may be used to determine maximum loss limits based on recent trading performance and current market conditions.
It is in no way intended as financial advice. Trade at your own risk.
Begin by adjusting the Account Equity slider to match your account size. This sets the baseline for you loss limits.
Adjust Recent Performance, Market Conditions, and Setup Quality to reflect actual performance and market conditions.
Thresholds for loss limits are customizable. Change these values to suit your current risk parameters. Default is between 1 and 3% of account equity.
Trade risk allocations are updated dynamically to reflect all respective variables.
